# Ideation Lenses

Use 4-7 lenses per session. Mix opposing lenses to avoid local optima.

## 1. First-Principles
Strip conventions. Ask: what must be true for success?

## 2. Inversion
Ask: how would this fail badly? Then design against it.

## 3. Constraint-First
Treat fixed constraints as design inputs, not blockers.

## 4. Experiment-First
Prioritize options that can be tested in <= 7 days.

## 5. Audience-Backwards
Start from the user/audience pain and desired behavior.

## 6. System Leverage
Find one change that unlocks multiple downstream benefits.

## 7. Analogy Transfer
Borrow patterns from adjacent domains and adapt.

## 8. Zero-Based Design
If starting from scratch today, what would be different?

## 9. Reversibility
Prefer high-learning options with low irreversible downside.

## 10. Long-Term Compounding
Favor options that improve with repetition and reuse.
